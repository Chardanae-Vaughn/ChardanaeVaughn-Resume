I'm a current second year student at UCR hoping to graduate within the next two years and embark on my journey to becoming a bestselling author of my Young Adult Fantasy series. I've been writing novels since I was in the second grade, so writing has become one of my many passiosns. I enjoy weaving worlds into worlds and creating characters and playing with all sorts of concepts and themes; I look forward to delving into the lower and upper division courses for my major so I can learn more about the craft of creative writing in fiction.

I was born and raised in the Bay Area in California where I live with all six of my siblings and my parents. I've always excelled in my classes all throughout my schooling career and hope for this trend to continue for the duration of my college career. With my siblings, I love playing video games, so as well as becoming a bestselling author, I also hope to one day be able to become a Twitch streamer and potentially even a Youtuber.

I've always been a hardworking student who, despite continuously facing off against procrastination, always gets her work done and turned in on time. Even through periods of self-doubt, I am ultimately determined to achieve all I desire in life and make a name for myself. I am introverted but willing to step outside my comfort zone if given the opportunity. Furthermore, I'm a very creative and open-minded individual, and work well with others.
